GoldenLaundry Pickup/Delivery Calculator

Calculate the cost to pick up or deliver your laundry from 28 Mafunka Crescent, Khayelitsha.

How to use:
1. Type your address in the search box below the map or tap "Use My Location".
2. Choose "Get Cost (One-Way)" for pickup or delivery, or "Get Cost (Round-Trip)" for both.
3. See the distance and cost below.
Cost: R25 for up to 2km, plus R5 per km beyond 2km. Round-trip doubles the one-way cost.
